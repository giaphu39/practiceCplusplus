Count the number of days from the start of this year (1/1) to today(dd/mm).

Input Format

A line of 2 integers: dd and mm. In which dd is the day and mm is the month in the current year.

Constraints

dd and mm are valid dates.

Output Format

The number of days from the start of the year to dd/mm.

Sample Input 0

17 10
Sample Output 0

289
Sample Input 1

31 9
Sample Output 1

NA
